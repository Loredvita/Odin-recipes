Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

This shake is ideal to refresh your brain from the jams you may have programming, it will give you extra power!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

    Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
