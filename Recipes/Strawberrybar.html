Delicious Strawberry Oatmeal Cookie Bars!

These bars are ideal for a programming environment, easy to make and do not get your hands dirty when you eat them!

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup packed light brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup seedless strawberry jam

    Directions
  1. Step 1
    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch square pan; line with greased foil.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Rub in butter using your hands or a pastry blender to form a crumbly mixture.

  3. Step 3

    4. Press 2 cups oat mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Spread jam to within 1/4 inch of the edge. Sprinkle remaining crumb mixture on top; press down lightly into jam.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 35 to 40 minutes. Allow to cool before cutting into bars.
